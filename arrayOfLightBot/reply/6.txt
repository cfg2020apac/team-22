JA Innovation Camp is a full-day JA experiential program that builds STEM and soft skills (creativity, entrepreneurial mindset, teamwork and problem solving) of students, and inspire them to apply their learnings to develop a solution for a problem that they identify in their communities through team exercise, prototyping and presentation. Students will be motivated to take initiatives to find innovative solutions to real-life problems and contribute to the community.
Program length: 10 hours (OLE learning time)
Target participants: Form 4 to Form 6 
Language: English or Cantonese
Program Format:
Face to face program: Full-day workshop led by business volunteers in a host school
Online program: Two half-day workshops led by business volunteers and conducted at online platforms
