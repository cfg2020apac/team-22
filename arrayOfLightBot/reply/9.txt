The program helps students develop a responsible attitude towards personal spending. Business volunteers share with the students their financial management experiences to help students understand the concepts of income, saving, expense, donation, budget and differentiate between “needs” and “wants”.
Program length: 1.5 hours (OLE learning time)
Target participants: Form 2 to Form 3 students
Class size: 100 students or above
Language: English or Cantonese
Program Format: 
Face-to-face program: Minimum one whole form of students, led by business volunteers at school hall
Online program: Minimum one whole form of students, led by business volunteers and conducted at online platforms
