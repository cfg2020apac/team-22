With engaging games and technology and activities led by business volunteers, the program increases students’ knowledge, skills and experience with money to promote self-confidence, and develops their financial capability and personal resiliency.
Program length (OLE learning time): 4.5 hours
Target participants: Form 1 to 4
Class size: 25 – 30 students
Language: English or Cantonese
Program Format:
Face-to-face program: 25-30 students per class, led by business volunteers in school
Online program: 25-30 students per class, led by business volunteers at online platforms
